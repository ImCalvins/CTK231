Who Is Calvin?

My name is Calvin Shandrow. I'm working toward a bachelor's degree in audio and music production at Illinois State University, and I currently have an associate's degree in art. I've been working on sound design at my church for over 6 years, and I'm normally in charge of sound during services. I've also been producing my own tracks for 2.5 years with a focus on environmental music for Dungeons and Dragons and electronic music for video games.
