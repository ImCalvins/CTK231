Vending:

A track I made for a friend's game. You played as a little robot trying to climb to the top of a town in the distant future while fighting enemies. It was made to be played in the shop, which itself was a vending machine. The song also featured an amped up version for when there was a flash sale in which all the items would be half price; I incorporated that version into this mix.
